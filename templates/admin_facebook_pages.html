How It Works
  1. Click "Connect New Facebook Page" to authorize with Facebook
  2. Select which pages you want to connect to this CRM inbox
  3. When someone fills out a Lead Ad on your page, it automatically creates a lead in your CRM
  4. The lead is assigned to your sales queue and notifications are sent to your team
Setup in Facebook

After connecting your pages:

  1. Go to your Lead Ads Form in Facebook Ads Manager
  2. Find the "Integrations" or "CRM" section
  3. Add webhook URL: {{ url_for('api_webhook_meta_ads', admin_identifier='YOUR_PAGE_ID', _external=True) }}
  4. Meta will verify the webhook. Your CRM will automatically acknowledge it
  5. That's it! Leads will start flowing into your CRM
